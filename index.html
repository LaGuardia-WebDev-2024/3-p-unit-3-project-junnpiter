── ⭑ UNITY DANCE CREW ⭑ ──


Unity Dance Crew is a K-Pop dance club at LaGuardia High School. Over each semester, students cover different K-Pop dances and gather together to perform at the end of the school year in the school's Little Flower Theater.
Students can either teach a song or audition for a dance and they collaborate together in order to complete the cover on time for the "evaluations" and the end of the year showcase.

You're probably wondering: What do you mean evaluations?
No, the board does not literally evaluate what you're doing and break it down piece by piece. It's just to show off what the groups worked on over the semester and to see if you managed to complete the dance cover within a timely manner.
